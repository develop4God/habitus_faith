English

Habitus Faith

Cultivate your spiritual life through daily discipline.

Habitus Faith is a mobile application built with Flutter designed to help you track and manage your spiritual habits. From daily prayer to Bible reading, this app provides the tools you need to stay consistent in your walk with God.

Features

Habit Tracking: Custom routines for your spiritual growth.

Prayer Journal: Keep track of your requests and gratitude.

Progress Insights: Visualize your consistency over time.

Clean Architecture: Built with Riverpod for robust state management.
